# Privacy Policy for TubeLoad

**Last updated: 15 May 2026**

TubeLoad is a fully offline structural calculator. It does not
collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** TubeLoad runs entirely on your device. It does not
connect to the internet, use analytics, or have any backend servers.

## What we store on your device
The following preferences are saved locally and never leave your
device:
- Unit preference (metric/imperial)
- Material selection
- Design factor
- Tube presets you save
- Display preferences

This data is sandboxed to the app and removed automatically if you
uninstall TubeLoad.

## Third-party services
TubeLoad does not use any third-party services, analytics,
advertising networks, or data brokers.

## Children's privacy
TubeLoad does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age,
including children under 13.
